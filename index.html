This Project

Started as a basic portfolio where I would display my projects mainly for me to learn the basics of html, css & java script but now it has become my autobiography; my personal life story. I tried to remember my life till now and let you feel & visualize it in phases. While making my timeline I realized what a huge mistake me and my family have made by not taking pictures and videos of the places where we lived and even the ones that we took of ourself are lost as our old phones stopped working and are not with us today. The photos and videos I used in this project are taken from the internet and the photos of my childhood are from the physical albums we luckily have. I will try to keep it up to date or maybe create a better one in future. This life story is what I won't tell people in general conversation because maybe we don't get that much time usually and because every single living thing has its own story; and we all are the main characters of our own movie. so just like I don't want to know everyone's story, why would I tell everyone about mine. This is mainly for my self and if someone (maybe you) really wants to know me more (but why?), they can read it. In case someone asks me about my story or they want to know me and I didn't had the time, I would send them th link to this site. Ok bruh you can read it now.
